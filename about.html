F.A.Q

  • What is Fleet Management

    Fleet management is the management of all the vehicles in a fleet from acquisition and resale to maintenance, vehicle tracking, fuel, accident management and more. Individuals, companies, organizations and government agencies (non-profit, education, services) need to transport self or staff from one location to the other. Vehicles are typically one of the largest operating costs. Cabiride rely on economies of scale to bring down the cost for all parties.

  • What are the minimum requirements to enjoy your services?

    To rent a car, you must be 21 years or older, have a valid drivers license and must be vetted by our team. for car management you need a road worthy car of 2005 and above that has been given a clean medical bill of health by our team of mechanics. For professional driver and delivery the are open to anyone.

  • What if i dont have time to visit your office cani still enjoy your services?

    Yes, our services can be requested online via this website from picking a product from our services page, we will reach out to you and come to your door step to serve you.

  • Do you operate in all 36 states including the FCT?

    No, for now we only operate in Lagos and the FCT, but we can deliver your goods from this two location to anywhere in Nigeria.

  • How are payments made?

    Payments are made directly into your nigeria account, every monday or first monday of every month if you select the monthly option.

  • Who handles repairs of cars?

    Repairs are handled by the owner of the car, however the company contirbutes a token depending on your plan/prior arrangeemnt monthly to ensure that your car is always healthy.
